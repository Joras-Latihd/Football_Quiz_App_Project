# ⚽ Football Quiz with Saroj
A feature-rich and responsive **Football Quiz App** built by **SAROJ DHITAL** using **React.js** and deployed on Vercel.

🔗 Live Demo:  
https://saroj-football-quiz.vercel.app/

This application challenges users with 500 football trivia questions across 3 difficulty modes, complete with a ranking system, lifelines, leaderboard, and a stunning Stadium Nights dark UI.

## 🚀 Core Features

### ✅ 500-Question Database
- 150 Easy questions (30s timer, 10 pts each)
- 250 Medium questions (25s timer, 20 pts each)
- 100 Hard questions (20s timer, 30 pts each)
- 9 categories: Players, Clubs, Trophies, Competitions, National Teams, Transfers, Managers, Records, History

### ✅ Authentication System
- Secure login with session management
- Protected quiz routes

### ✅ Quiz Engine
- 15 randomly selected questions per session
- Live countdown timer with colour indicator (green → yellow → red)
- Streak tracking with bonus points for fast answers
- -5 point penalty for wrong answers

### ✅ 3 Lifelines (One use each per session)
- ✂️ 50/50 — Eliminates 2 wrong answers
- ⏭️ Skip — Skip question with no penalty
- ⏱️ +15s — Adds 15 seconds to the timer

### ✅ 8 Unique Rank System
- ⭐ World Champion (95%+)
- 🥇 Ballon d'Or Winner (85%+)
- 🦺 Club Captain (75%+)
- 📋 First Team Regular (60%+)
- 🔄 Rotation Player (45%+)
- 🪑 Professional Bench Warmer (30%+)
- 🧒 Academy Prospect (15%+)
- 🚪 You've Been Released (0%+)

### ✅ Leaderboard
- Top 10 scores saved per difficulty mode
- Persistent across sessions via localStorage

### ✅ Answer Review
- Full review of all 15 questions after quiz
- Shows correct answers and educational explanations

### ✅ Responsive Design
- Works on desktop, tablet and mobile devices
- Stadium Nights dark aesthetic with animated UI

---

## 📘 How It Works

1. Open the application.
2. Login with your quiz credentials.
3. Choose a difficulty mode — Easy, Medium or Hard.
4. Answer 15 random questions against the clock.
5. Use lifelines strategically to maximise your score.
6. See your rank and review your answers.
7. Compete for the top spot on the leaderboard.
